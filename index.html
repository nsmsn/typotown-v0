Welcome to Typo.town

A personal resource on typography. For now there are two lists: Google Fonts and Adobe Fonts.

My intention for Typo.town is to be a resource for discovering and selecting great typography. This will likely take the shape of a blog, a wiki, or maybe something in between.

There are many, many great type resources out there on the web, but I’ve found my favorites have a more personal touch. I’m thinking about Jeremiah Shoaf’s Typewolf and Bethany Heck’s Font Review Journal in particular. With Typo.town, I hope to share more of the why rather than the how. I expect it will be a bit personal and idiosyncratic. I invite you to come on this journey with me. Stay tuned! ✌️
